Test Instructions

Important Notice About Test Attempts:

  • Maximum Attempts: You can attempt this test a maximum of 2 times.
  • Rankings & Percentile: Only your first attempt will be included in rankings and percentile calculations.
  • Second Attempt: Your second attempt is for practice and review. It will be saved and you can view solutions, but it will not affect your rank.
  • Why This Policy? This ensures fairness for all students, as second attempts may benefit from remembering questions and answers from the first attempt.

How Our App Works

1. Timer

The countdown timer at the top right corner will display the remaining time available to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the test will automatically end.

2. Question Palette & Status Symbols

The Question Palette on the right side shows the status of each question using the following symbols:

White/Empty Box: You have not visited this question yet.
✓
Red Circle: You have not answered this question.
✓
Green Circle: You have answered this question.
✓
Light Purple Circle: You have NOT answered the question, but marked it for review.
✓
Dark Purple Circle: You have answered the question AND marked it for review.

3. Navigating a Question

  • Click on a question number in the Question Palette to navigate directly to that question.
  • Click "Save & Next" to save your answer and move to the next question.
  • Click "Mark for Review & Next" to save and mark a question for review, then move to the next.

⚠️ Important: Your answer will NOT be saved if you navigate directly without clicking Save or Mark for Review.

4. Answering a Question

To answer a multiple choice question:

  • Choose one answer from the 4 options (A, B, C, D)
  • Click the bubble before your chosen option to select it
  • To deselect your answer, click the bubble again or click "Clear Response"
