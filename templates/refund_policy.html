Refund Policy

No Refunds on Completed Purchases
All payments made on examPattern for test series subscriptions are final and non-refundable. Once a purchase is confirmed and your test series is activated, we do not offer refunds, partial credits, or exchanges under any circumstances.

1. Why We Have a No-Refund Policy

examPattern provides digital educational content — specifically, access to mock tests, question banks, and detailed solutions. Because this content is made available to you immediately upon payment confirmation, it cannot be "returned" like a physical product. To maintain fairness for all students and to sustain our platform, all sales are final.

2. What Is Covered Under This Policy

  • One-time payments for individual test series subscriptions.
  • Any future subscription plans or bundled packages offered on examPattern.
  • Payments made through Razorpay or any other payment gateway integrated on this platform.

3. Exception — Payment Deducted but Test Series Not Activated

Payment Failure / Activation Issue?
If your money was deducted but your test series was not activated in your account, you are entitled to a full resolution. Please raise a support request as described below.

Technical failures can sometimes occur during payment processing — your bank may deduct the amount but our system may not receive a successful confirmation from the payment gateway. In such cases, we will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.

How to raise a request:

1

Email us at prathibhainstitutions@gmail.com with the subject line: "Payment Deducted — Test Series Not Activated".

2

Include your registered email address, the Razorpay Payment ID (found in your bank SMS or email receipt), the name of the test series you purchased, and the date and amount of the transaction.

3

Our team will verify the transaction with Razorpay and check your account status. We will respond within 2–3 business days.

4

If the payment is confirmed but the activation failed on our end, we will immediately activate your test series or initiate a full refund, whichever you prefer.

  Resolution typically takes 2–3 business days from the date we receive your request. Refunds, if applicable, are processed back to your original payment method and may take an additional 3–5 business days to reflect depending on your bank.

4. Duplicate Payments

If you were charged more than once for the same order due to a technical error, please contact us at prathibhainstitutions@gmail.com with proof of both transactions. After verification, the duplicate charge will be fully refunded within 5–7 business days.

5. Subscription Cancellations

Currently, examPattern offers one-time access purchases (not recurring subscriptions). If subscription plans are introduced in the future, a separate cancellation policy will be published and users will be notified in advance.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
